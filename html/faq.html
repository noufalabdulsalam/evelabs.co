Frequently Asked Questions?

What is Dripo smart infusion monitor system ?

It is a pack of infusion devices that connected to a centralised hub at the Nursing station. The device monitor the course of infusions and will update status of every infusion in the hub. The device also has a fine rate controller or adjusting flow-rate.

Does this device come in contact with the infusion fluid ?

No, Dripo device is externally clamped to the drip chamber of any IV set with drip chamber diameter between 20 mm and 12 mm.

How is the flow rate controlled ?

Dripo has a mechanical fine flow regulator that compress the IV tube to control rate of flow

Who can access the data on dripo.

Only registered user of the Dripo smart infusion system can access the data specific to that system.

Can I access and monitor the infusion data on my mobile or laptop remotely?

Yes, you can access monitor the data of infusion by logging in to our online portal www.dripo.care.

When will dripo be available in the Market ?

Dripo will be available for Indian customers by July 2017.

Will the device work when there is no internet connectivity ?

Yes the device has an offline mode as a standalone infusion monitor.
